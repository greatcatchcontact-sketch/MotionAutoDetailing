Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a detail take?

Express: ~1.5–2.5 hrs • Full: 3–5 hrs • Ceramic: 1–2 days (incl. cure time).

Do you offer mobile service?

Yes—within our service area. We need access to your driveway and a power outlet.

What prep is included with ceramic coatings?

Decon wash, clay bar, multi-stage correction, panel wipe, then coating application.
